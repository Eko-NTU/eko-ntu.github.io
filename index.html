About

Welcome

I am a Professor of Economics at the Economics Programme and Chair of the School of Social Sciences at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore.

My research primarily revolves around Experimental and Behavioral Economics. I study how people make decisions in strategic and market environments — including experimental asset markets and finance, market design for altruistic supply (such as organ donation), and the dynamics of cooperation and coordination in social networks.

I serve as an Associate Editor of the Journal of Economic Behavior and Organization (JEBO).
