Welcome

Yohanes Eko Riyanto
Professor of Economics | Chair, School of Social Sciences
Nanyang Technological University, Singapore

I am an experimental and behavioral economist. My research focuses on the understanding of the complexities of human decision-making within strategic and market environments. It integrates theoretical insights with experimental methodology and empirical rigor to explore how individuals navigate information and incentives.

My work has appeared in the Journal of Finance, Management Science, Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, Economic Journal, Journal of Economic Theory, Review of Economics and Statistics, Games and Economic Behavior, International Economic Review, European Economic Review, Experimental Economics, Journal of Economic Dynamics and Control, and Journal of Economic Behavior and Organization, among others.

Beyond my research, I serve as the Chair of the School of Social Sciences at NTU and as an Associate Editor for the Journal of Economic Behavior and Organization (JEBO).
